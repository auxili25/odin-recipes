Hamburger Recipes

Description

A perfect, juicy all American Hamburger! Here I’ll share 3 simple ways to cook burgers (including stovetop, grill and even oven), plus tips on how to build the best burger. This includes a simple homemade bun, a variety of sauces and all the best toppings! A perfect, juicy all American Hamburger! Ingredients

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 2 strips of bacon, diced
  • 1 cup white onion cut into slices
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 6 pieces of hamburger buns cut into halves
  • 1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 6 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 6 slices of tomato
  • 1 lettuce

Steps

  1. Season the meat with 1 tablespoon of MAGGI® Naturísimo Seasoning and the Worcestershire sauce. Add the bacon, mix well and divide the meat into six portions. Form balls and flatten them into a hamburger shape
  2. On a lightly oiled grill, sear the meat and cook over indirect heat on both sides. Booking.
  3. In a frying pan, heat the onion, water and sugar. Cook until caramelized, remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Separately mix the mayonnaise and 1 tablespoon of MAGGI® Naturísimo Seasoning. Prepare the burgers as usual with the rest of the ingredients. Serve with the onion, the reserved mayonnaise and the amount of lettuce leaves of your preference.
  5. Remove from the grill, cool and enjoy your Beef Burgers, with the unique flavor of MAGGI® Naturísimo Seasoning.
